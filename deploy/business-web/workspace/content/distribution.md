# Distribution System

## Goal

Turn one strong published article into multiple distribution assets.

## Per-article checklist

For each article marked `status: published`:

1. add 5-10 hooks to `content/shorts.md`
2. note one newsletter angle
3. note one thread angle
4. note one lead magnet or paid-product angle
5. update `business/channel-plan.md` if a platform deserves more focus

## Channel principles

- website first
- newsletter second
- short-form derivatives third
- distribution should amplify the thesis, not distort it

## Quality bar

- one clear idea per asset
- no filler intros
- no synthetic “viral” tone
- every derivative should still be useful on its own
